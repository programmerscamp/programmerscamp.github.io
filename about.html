About Programmer's Camp



By Programmers, For Programmers

Brief History

Programmer's Camp was created as a small online group by a young, dedicated programmer called Prince. The Group quickly started growing and is now a small community of passionate programmers of various fields.

Main Goal

Programmer's Camp was created with the following goals in mind:

  • Learn from each other
  • Teach each other
  • Birthplace for ideas
  • Ideas to reality

All of this is done in a fun environment where everyone can enjoy while they code beacuse what's the point of doing something when you're not having the time of your life.

-Ideas are to be respected no matter the age. Ideas are created by the "mind". They don't require a specific mindset or maturity level.-

How Ideas are worked on

Any member of the group proposes his/her idea and members of the group discuss the idea. Programmers of the related field work together to then create the idea while other members look at the development and learn from it. All of the projects are open source and can be found on the group's Github.
